Hello, I'm Gilbert from Lausanne, CH

After more than 6 years of passionate and intense work in Sophia Genetics, I decided to go back to freelance activity. With an initial master's degree in science (fluid mechanics and thermodynamics), followed some years later by a master's degree in computer science, I have always been passionated by the visual representation of data: how to tell a story with data and how to combine art and design.

Outside of work I love Photography and running. See my previous work history on LinkedIn.

How I can help you

Data Visualisation

I design and create different kind of visual representations: from complex interactive (exploratory data visualisation) to static posters or infographics or "dashboard" views.

Either on small datasets or large datasets, it may also include data pre-processing (acquisition, data cleaning, data wrangling, exploratory analysis, ...).

My goal: combine both aesthetic form and functionality to communicate information clearly and efficiently.

D3.js, HTML Canvas, Nodebox3, Processing, javascript, python, ...

Frontend Engineering

I design and develop Frontend Applications (UI), either at components level (visual components integrated into an existing application) or the complete client application.

Frontend "engineering" include also writing complex algorithms, unit tests, and care about performances and page/bundle size to manage more and more complex user interfaces.

Targetted platforms: Web and Java.

HTML Javascript CSS, Java/JavaFX, Node.js/Express, Java/Spring

UI/UX Design

I use Design Thinking Approach to elaborate user interactions in the context of the multiple aspects of a product (not only in terms of interaction design or usability & accessibility, but also in terms of business, brand, strategy, ...).

I develop quickly UI prototypes (interactive or static) or sketches to support this iterative approach and refine my ideas based on stakeholders feedbacks.

My goal: constantly paying attention to details!

Balsamiq Mockup, D3.js, Observable HQ, Nodebox3 and my handwritten notebook!
